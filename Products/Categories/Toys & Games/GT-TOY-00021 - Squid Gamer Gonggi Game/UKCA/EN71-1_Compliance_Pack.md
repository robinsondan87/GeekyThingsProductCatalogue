## 3) EN71-1 Compliance Section for UKCA Technical File

**Product Name:** Squid Gamer Gonggi Game
**SKU(s):** GT-TOY-10050
**Material:** PLA (SunLu PLA 2.0+)
**Intended Age Group:** 3 years and over

---

### 3.1 Product Description

The product is a 3D-printed articulated sensory fidget toy manufactured from PLA. The toy consists of interlinked articulated segments forming a single integral structure. It contains no electronics, no magnets, and no metal components.

The product is intended for children aged 3 years and over.

---

### 3.2 Applicable Standard

EN 71-1:2014 + A1:2018
Safety of Toys - Mechanical and Physical Properties

---

### 3.3 Age Grading Rationale

The product is not intended for children under 36 months due to:

* Presence of articulated joints
* Potential creation of small parts after damage

The product is labelled accordingly with:
"Not suitable for children under 36 months. Small parts."

---

### 3.4 Mechanical and Physical Testing

The following mechanical safety checks were performed in line with EN71-1 principles for toys intended for children aged 3+:

* Visual inspection for sharp edges and points
* Drop test from 1.0 m onto a hard surface (3 repetitions)
* Manual tension and torsion testing of articulated joints

---

### 3.5 Test Results

During testing, the product either withstood mechanical stress or fractured in a non-hazardous manner.

Where fracture occurred:

* No sharp edges were created
* No sharp points were created
* No brittle shards or splinters were produced
* No dangerous projections were formed

Any resulting parts were blunt and did not present additional mechanical hazards beyond small-parts ingestion risk.

---

### 3.6 Compliance Conclusion

Based on the above testing and inspections, the product is considered to comply with the mechanical and physical safety requirements of EN 71-1 for toys intended for children aged 3 years and over.
